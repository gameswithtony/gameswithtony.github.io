Important Notes

  • The app will refer to spaces in locations by number, for example "Coalbrookdale (Space 2)". The spaces are numbered from left-to-right, top-to-bottom. See the below reference:
  • The app will explain how to setup the game. You will tell the app what you are doing, and the app will tell you what the solo opponents do. We recommend telling the app what you're doing as you do it.
  • If you think the app is making a mistake, use the 'View Board State' link at the top of the app to compare your physical board with the app's.
  • GAMEPLAY DIFFERENCES:
    • For coal consumption, the AIs always behave as if they are connected to the coal market (they must be actually connected to move coal into the market).
    • The AIs do not require beer to sell, but they will consume it if they can. If they cannot sell they attempt a free network action. They also earn extra VP under certain circumstances (the app will tell you).
    • You play by normal rules, except you cannot overbuild (the AIs can't either).
    • Removing industry tiles to pay for negative income is not currently supported.

4) Place Eliza's tiles (use Gray tiles) next to the board. You may setup a player board for her if you wish, but it is not necessary. Eliza does not use Level 1 industries nor Level 2 manufactured goods. These are all considered already developed.

5) Place Eleanor's tiles (use Yellow tiles) next to the board. You may setup a player board for her if you wish, but it is not necessary. Eleanor does not use Level 1 industries nor Level 2 manufactured goods. These are all considered already developed.
